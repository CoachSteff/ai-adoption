## Emergency Backup Plans

### If Tech Fails
1. **Use DEMO_SCRIPT.md** to walk through content verbally
2. **Show source Markdown files** as backup
3. **Focus on discussion** and show content later via email

### If Time Runs Short
**Priority order:**
1. Business Owner Checklist deep dive (7 min)
2. Feedback discussion (10 min)
3. Skip: Full site navigation, workshop details

### If Audience is Skeptical
1. **Acknowledge**: "I appreciate the skepticism - AI hype is real"
2. **Focus**: "Let me show you the most practical piece"
3. **Invite criticism**: "What would make this credible for you?"

### If They Love It Too Much
1. **Manage expectations**: "This is v1.0 - gaps exist"
2. **Be honest**: "Interactive tools coming in v1.1"
3. **Focus feedback**: "What matters most for your immediate needs?"
