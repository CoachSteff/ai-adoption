## Webinar Series Adaptation

### Condensed Format (60-90 minutes each)

Each workshop can be adapted as a webinar with this structure:

**Webinar Format:**
1. **Introduction** (5 min) - Welcome, agenda, housekeeping
2. **Core Content** (30-40 min) - Key frameworks and concepts
3. **Demo/Walkthrough** (10-15 min) - Show playbook resources
4. **Q&A** (10-15 min) - Interactive discussion
5. **Call-to-Action** (5 min) - Next steps, resources, follow-up

**Webinar Enhancements:**
- Pre-webinar survey (gather participant context)
- Live polls during session (increase engagement)
- Chat-based Q&A (capture all questions)
- Post-webinar resources (links, handouts, recordings)
- Follow-up email sequence (nurture engagement)

## Workshop Quality Standards

### Participant Experience
- Clear learning objectives communicated upfront
- Interactive elements every 15-20 minutes
- Relevant examples and case studies
- Actionable takeaways (not just theory)
- Post-workshop support available

### Facilitator Preparation
- Rehearse full workshop at least once
- Review all materials 24 hours before
- Test technology setup 1 hour before
- Have backup plans for common issues
- Follow up with participants within 48 hours

### Content Quality
- Evidence-based (cited sources)
- Current (reviewed quarterly)
- Accessible (clear language, no jargon)
- Inclusive (diverse examples, perspectives)
- Actionable (practical frameworks, tools)
