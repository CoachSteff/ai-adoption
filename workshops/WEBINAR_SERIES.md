**Platform:** Zoom / LinkedIn Live / YouTube Live
**Target Audience:** Business leaders, executives, HR partners, technical leaders

## Technical Setup

### Platform Recommendations

**Option A: Zoom Webinar** (Recommended for <1000 participants)
- ✅ Excellent interaction (polls, Q&A, chat)
- ✅ Breakout rooms for small group work
- ✅ Easy recording and transcription
- ✅ Registration and reminder automation
- ⚠️ Cost: Paid plan required for webinars

**Option B: LinkedIn Live** (Best for professional audience)
- ✅ Native audience on LinkedIn
- ✅ Easy sharing and amplification
- ✅ Professional networking built-in
- ⚠️ Limited interaction features
- ⚠️ No registration/email capture

**Option C: YouTube Live** (Best for broad reach)
- ✅ Unlimited audience size
- ✅ Evergreen content (always available)
- ✅ SEO and discoverability
- ⚠️ Minimal interaction features
- ⚠️ Requires separate registration

**Hybrid Approach:**
- Primary: Zoom Webinar (interactive session)
- Secondary: Simulcast to LinkedIn/YouTube (broader reach)
- Post-event: Recording on YouTube (evergreen)

---

### Technical Checklist

**Pre-Webinar (1 Week Before):**
- [ ] Test platform and all features
- [ ] Prepare slides and screen shares
- [ ] Test microphone and camera
- [ ] Set up co-host/moderator access
- [ ] Create polls and Q&A templates
