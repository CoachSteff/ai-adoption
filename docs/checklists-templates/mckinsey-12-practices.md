# McKinsey's 12 Adoption & Scaling Practices for EBIT Impact

This list functions as a final summary of the entire playbook. An organization that can check all 12 is a high-performer.

## The 12 Practices

### 1. Dedicated Team
- [ ] Is there a team driving adoption?
- [ ] Team has clear mandate and authority
- [ ] Team includes cross-functional representation
- [ ] Team has executive sponsorship

### 2. Regular Communication
- [ ] Are you building momentum by sharing value created?
- [ ] Regular updates on AI adoption progress
- [ ] Success stories shared across organization
- [ ] Communication is two-way (listening to feedback)

### 3. Senior Leaders Engaged
- [ ] Are leaders actively driving and role-modeling AI use?
- [ ] Leaders personally using AI tools
- [ ] Leaders visible in AI initiatives
- [ ] Leadership commitment clear and consistent

### 4. Embed in Business Processes
- [ ] Are you redesigning workflows, not just adding tools?
- [ ] AI integrated into core business processes
- [ ] Workflows redesigned around AI capabilities
- [ ] Process changes documented and communicated

### 5. Role-Based Training
- [ ] Is your upskilling tailored?
- [ ] Training customized by role
- [ ] Training aligned with AI maturity level
- [ ] Training includes hands-on practice

### 6. Foster Employee Trust
- [ ] Do you have a plan for the "New EVP" and mitigating "silent impacts"?
- [ ] EVP addresses AI era needs
- [ ] Plan to mitigate burnout, loneliness, loss of agency
- [ ] Transparent about AI's impact on roles

### 7. Feedback Mechanism
- [ ] Is there a loop to improve AI solutions over time?
- [ ] Regular feedback collection from users
- [ ] Feedback incorporated into improvements
- [ ] Feedback loop is visible and responsive

### 8. Clear Road Map
- [ ] Do you have a 7-workstream plan?
- [ ] Roadmap covers all 7 workstreams:
  - [ ] AI Strategy
  - [ ] AI Value
  - [ ] AI Organization
  - [ ] AI People & Culture
  - [ ] AI Governance
  - [ ] AI Engineering
  - [ ] AI Data
- [ ] Roadmap is communicated and understood

### 9. Compelling Change Story
- [ ] Do employees know why?
- [ ] Change story connects to business objectives
- [ ] Change story addresses employee concerns
- [ ] Change story is consistently communicated

### 10. Track Well-Defined KPIs
- [ ] Are you tracking ROE and ROF, not just cost?
- [ ] KPIs defined and tracked
- [ ] KPIs cover ROI, ROE, and ROF
- [ ] Regular reporting on KPI performance

### 11. Employee Incentives
- [ ] Is adoption reinforced?
- [ ] Incentives aligned with AI adoption goals
- [ ] Recognition for AI-related achievements
- [ ] Performance reviews include AI adoption

### 12. Foster Customer Trust
- [ ] Are you compliant (EU AI Act) and transparent?
- [ ] EU AI Act compliance in place
- [ ] Transparency about AI use with customers
- [ ] Customer trust measures in place

## Scoring Your Organization

Count how many practices you've checked:

- **10-12 practices**: High-performer - You're in the top tier
- **7-9 practices**: Good progress - Focus on missing practices
- **4-6 practices**: Getting started - Prioritize foundational practices
- **0-3 practices**: Early stage - Start with practices 1, 3, and 8

## Priority Order

If you're just starting, focus on these first:

1. **Practice 8**: Clear Road Map (foundation for everything)
2. **Practice 3**: Senior Leaders Engaged (critical for success)
3. **Practice 1**: Dedicated Team (execution capability)
4. **Practice 9**: Compelling Change Story (employee buy-in)
5. **Practice 10**: Track Well-Defined KPIs (measurement)

## Next Steps

1. **Assess**: Score your organization on all 12 practices
2. **Prioritize**: Focus on practices with lowest scores
3. **Plan**: Create action plan for each practice
4. **Execute**: Implement improvements systematically
5. **Reassess**: Review quarterly to track progress
