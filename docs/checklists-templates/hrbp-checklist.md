# Actionable Checklist for the HR Business Partner (HRBP)

Use this checklist to ensure you're covering all critical areas for successful AI adoption from a human resources perspective.

## Change & Culture (Part 3)

### New EVP for the AI Era
- [ ] Developed a New Employee Value Proposition (EVP) for the AI Era
- [ ] Plan to study and mitigate the "silent impacts" of AI:
  - [ ] Burnout & Difficulty
  - [ ] Loneliness & Isolation
  - [ ] Loss of Agency
  - [ ] Loss of Learning
- [ ] EVP addresses how workers will share in the value AI creates
- [ ] EVP guarantees new learning opportunities to compensate for those lost to automation
- [ ] EVP empowers experimentation with psychological safety

### HR-Tech Partnership
- [ ] Core partner with the CTO in a "Chief Human and Machine Resource" capacity
- [ ] Regular collaboration meetings scheduled
- [ ] Co-ownership of AI adoption strategy established
- [ ] Joint decision-making on AI tool selection and deployment

### Change Management
- [ ] Developed a "compelling change story" based on McKinsey's "Rewired" playbook
- [ ] Vision and roadmap created with "shared vision of opportunity"
- [ ] Change management plan exists and is being executed
- [ ] Regular communication about AI's impact on roles and processes
- [ ] Employee concerns addressed proactively

### Psychological Safety
- [ ] Safe environment created for experimentation and learning
- [ ] Mistakes viewed as learning opportunities, not failures
- [ ] Open dialogue encouraged about AI fears and concerns
- [ ] Feedback mechanisms established

## Talent & Upskilling (Part 3.3)

### 6-Step Upskilling Framework
- [ ] **Step 1: Map Roles** - Identified which roles will be streamlined vs. enhanced
- [ ] **Step 2: Assess the Skills Gap** - Used surveys and AI-driven analytics to map existing skills against future needs
- [ ] **Step 3: Develop a Taxonomy of Skills** - Defined crucial new skills (prompt engineering, data literacy, AI ethics, human-AI collaboration)
- [ ] **Step 4: Design & Deploy Training** - Created role-based, tailored training programs (not one-size-fits-all)
- [ ] **Step 5: Measure Efficacy** - Tracking skills acquisition and application
- [ ] **Step 6: Iterate Continuously** - Established continuous learning function

### L&D Program Audit
- [ ] Audited current L&D programs
- [ ] Assessed if programs are "ready for the future" (target: be in the 21% with high-quality programs)
- [ ] Identified gaps in current programs
- [ ] Updated programs to address AI-related skills

### Training Delivery
- [ ] Training is "role-based" and tailored to AI maturity
- [ ] Mandatory training sessions delivered
- [ ] Department-level workshops conducted
- [ ] Peer learning systems implemented (15-minute teach-back model)
- [ ] Continuous learning pathways established

### EU AI Act Compliance (Article 20)
- [ ] AI literacy training provided to all employees using AI systems
- [ ] Training covers AI capabilities and limitations
- [ ] Training includes awareness of risks and ethical implications
- [ ] Training completion verified and documented

## Shadow AI Management (Part 3.4)

### Training & Education Component
- [ ] Partnering with Legal/IT to deliver "Train and Educate" component of Shadow AI Management Plan
- [ ] Employees trained on why Shadow AI is a risk
- [ ] Employees educated on approved AI tools
- [ ] Clear communication about acceptable use policies

### Support Innovation Framework
- [ ] Simple, accessible process for employees to request new AI tools
- [ ] Process is not bureaucratic or slow
- [ ] Sandbox environment available for testing
- [ ] Fast-track approval for low-risk tools

## Employee Engagement

### Early Involvement
- [ ] Employees actively involved from the beginning of AI adoption
- [ ] Employee feedback incorporated into solutions
- [ ] Frontrunners and champions identified and empowered
- [ ] Employee success stories shared publicly

### Communication
- [ ] Transparent communication about AI capabilities and limitations
- [ ] Regular updates on AI adoption progress
- [ ] Clear explanation of how AI will augment, not replace
- [ ] Open channels for questions and concerns

### Recognition & Rewards
- [ ] Successes publicly celebrated
- [ ] Efforts of pilot teams acknowledged
- [ ] Employee incentives aligned with AI adoption goals
- [ ] Recognition programs updated to include AI-related achievements

## Measurement

### Employee Metrics
- [ ] Employee satisfaction tracked
- [ ] Skills acquisition measured
- [ ] Training effectiveness evaluated
- [ ] Adoption rates monitored
- [ ] Employee feedback collected regularly

### Business Impact
- [ ] ROE (Return on Employee) tracked
- [ ] Employee productivity measured
- [ ] Retention rates monitored
- [ ] Employee engagement scores tracked

## Next Steps

1. **Review**: Check off completed items
2. **Prioritize**: Focus on unchecked items that are blocking progress
3. **Plan**: Create action plan with timelines
4. **Engage**: Involve employees in planning and execution
5. **Measure**: Track progress and adjust as needed
