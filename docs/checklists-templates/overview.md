# Actionable Checklists for Executive Stakeholders

This section translates the playbook's frameworks into actionable, role-based checklists. Use these to ensure you're covering all critical areas for successful AI adoption.

## Role-Based Checklists

### [CAIO / CTO Checklist](/docs/checklists-templates/cto-checklist)
For technical leaders responsible for governance, compliance, and scaling.

**Covers**:
- Governance & Compliance (EU AI Act, NIS2, TRiSM)
- Scaling & Engineering (ModelOps, Data Governance)
- Agentic AI governance

### [HR Business Partner Checklist](/docs/checklists-templates/hrbp-checklist)
For HR leaders managing the human side of AI adoption.

**Covers**:
- Change & Culture (EVP, silent impacts, change story)
- Talent & Upskilling (6-Step Framework, L&D audit)
- Shadow AI training and education

### [Business Owner / Unit Leader Checklist](/docs/checklists-templates/business-owner-checklist)
For business leaders prioritizing use cases and measuring value.

**Covers**:
- Value & Prioritization (use case selection, KPI alignment)
- Measurement & ROI (ROE, ROF frameworks)
- Adoption & Governance (Shadow AI management)

## How to Use These Checklists

1. **Review**: Read through the checklist for your role
2. **Assess**: Mark what you've already completed
3. **Prioritize**: Identify the most critical unchecked items
4. **Plan**: Create action items for unchecked items
5. **Track**: Revisit quarterly to measure progress

## Customization

These checklists are starting points. Customize them based on:
- Your organization's maturity level
- Industry-specific requirements
- Regulatory environment
- Organizational priorities
