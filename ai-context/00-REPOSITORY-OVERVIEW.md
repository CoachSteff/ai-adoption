## Core Mission

This repository provides a comprehensive, human-centric framework for AI adoption in EU enterprises. Unlike vendor playbooks that focus on technology, this guide addresses the **real barrier to AI success**: the human element.

**Key Principle**: AI Adoption is a **human behavior change challenge**, not just a technical integration problem.

## Problem Statement

Enterprise AI adoption in 2025 faces a critical paradox:
- **95%** of organizations report zero measurable ROI from AI investments
- **42%** abandon AI projects before production (up from 17% the previous year)
- **81%** of adoption failures are human-centric (leadership + training issues)

This is not a technology failure—it's a **human adoption failure**.
